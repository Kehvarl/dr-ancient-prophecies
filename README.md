# Ancient Prohphecies
## 2026 Ancient and Nameless and Fun and Stupid Game Jam entry
---
What would you do if you found a mysterious deck of cards?  If you thought "challenge my friends to a few rounds of Higher-or-Lower", then you're probably already under their control; congratulations!

The rules of the game are simple:  A card is revealed, you predict if the next card will be higher or lower than the one revealed.  If you're right, you gain points.  If you're wrong you don't.

Oh, and ties just get skipped over, so don't worry about them.

Play 5 rounds of Higher or Lower and see just how good your future sight is.  Maybe you'll even learn something about things yet to come.
